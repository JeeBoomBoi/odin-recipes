Lassi

Description

Lassi, a creamy, frothy yogurt-based drink, blended with water and various fruits or seasonings (such as salt or sugar), that originated in Punjab, India.

Ingredients

  • yogurt
  • ice cubes
  • ice water
  • 2 tsps white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. In a blender, blend the yogurt, ice, water, sugar and salt until mixture becomes frothy. Pour mixture over ice cubes in tall glasses.
